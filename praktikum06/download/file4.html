The Open Group's Platinum Members















The Open Group is a vendor-neutral and technology-neutral
consortium, whose vision of Boundaryless Information Flow™
will enable access to integrated information, within and among enterprises,
based on open standards and global interoperability.
WHAT'S NEW
  • The Open Group welcomed two more new members at the end of April: Biner Consulting (Sweden) and Enterprise Architects Pty Ltd (Australia) which have both joined the Architecture Forum.They, together with MIC Business Solutions, Inc. (USA), eflow.Inc (Japan), Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA) and Soluta.net (Italy) bring to seven the number of new members joining The Open Group in the second half of the month.
